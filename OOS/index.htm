Introduction

Builders Steel Company has been fabricating and erecting structural steel since 1927. Our experience and knowledge has given us the capacity to handle all facets of structural steel construction. We are well known in the industry as a structural steel fabricator and erector that consistently meets or exceeds contract requirements and client expectations. Our employees are integral to our success. Through their loyalty, knowledge and dedication we have been able to blend a personal approach to business with state of the art construction methods. We believe that a high standard of quality is essential to our objectives and the continued growth and success of the company. Builders Steel Company has earned the American Institute of Steel Construction (AISC) “Standard for Steel Building Structures”, the “Standard for Bridge and Highway Metal Components”, and the “Advanced Certified Steel Erector with Metal Deck Installation Endorsement” Certifications.

Mission Statement

"To be the Structural Steel Contractor of Choice"

Our mission is to provide a quality product, competitively priced and delivered on schedule with courtesy and professionalism.
















Services

Fabrication

Our fabrication facility, located in North Kansas City, MO includes a 70,000 sq. ft. structural shop and a 30,000 sq. ft. miscellaneous and ornamental shop, all under roof. Utilization of CNC equipment allows us to use the latest technologies to increase production capacity, improve efficiency and assure quality. Our AISC certification ensures quality and efficiencies in our shop which eliminates the need for outside inspections in most cases. We have successfully met the quality certification requirements for “Standard for Steel Building Structures” and the “Standard for Bridge and Highway Metal Components”. Our welders are all AWS certified and we have an AWS certified welding inspector on staff. Safety is an integral part of our fabrication process. Our safety programs are administered by a committee of management, shop employees and erection employees which keeps employees engaged.

Erection

Builders Steel Company utilizes its own erection crews which distinguishes us as a “Full Service Steel Subcontractor”. Our erection capabilities range from the smallest miscellaneous projects up to large commercial and industrial structural steel projects. Our AISC certification ensures quality and efficiencies in our erection processes. We have successfully met the quality certification requirements for “Standard for Certified Steel Erector (Advanced) with Metal Deck Installation Endorsement”. All ironworkers are members of the Ironworkers Local 10 and are required to complete an apprenticeship program through the Builders Association of Kansas City. Safety is an integral part of our erection process. Our safety programs are administered by a committee of management, shop employees and erection employees which keeps employees engaged.
