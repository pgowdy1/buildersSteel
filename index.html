Introduction

Builders Steel was founded in 1927 and has been employee owned since day one. AISC Certified for Complex Steel Buildings and Bridge Component -- Builders Steel operates in a 100,000 sq. ft. fabrication space and is experienced in Structural Steel Fabrication.

Mission Statement

"To be the Structural Steel Contractor of Choice"

Our mission is to provide a quality product, competitively priced and delivered on schedule with courtesy an professionalism while maintaining a reasonable profit.
